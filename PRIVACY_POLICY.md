# Privacy Policy for Contractor Vault

**Last Updated:** December 9, 2024

## Overview

Contractor Vault is a browser extension that enables secure, temporary session sharing between administrators and contractors. This privacy policy explains how we handle your data.

## Data Collection

### What We Collect
- **Session Cookies**: When you choose to share a session, the extension captures browser cookies for the specific website you're sharing access to.
- **Email Addresses**: Optional contractor email for access tracking.
- **Audit Logs**: Timestamps, actions taken, and IP addresses for security purposes.

### What We DON'T Collect
- Passwords
- Personal browsing history
- Data from sites you don't explicitly share
- Any data without your explicit action

## Data Usage

Collected data is used solely for:
1. Enabling temporary session sharing functionality
2. Automatic session expiration and cleanup
3. Security audit logging

## Data Storage

- Session data is encrypted using Fernet (AES-128-CBC) encryption
- Data is stored on our secure servers
- Sessions are automatically deleted after expiration

## Data Sharing

We do NOT sell, trade, or share your data with third parties.

## Your Rights

You can:
- Delete your data at any time by revoking sessions
- Request complete data deletion by contacting us
- Choose not to use the extension

## Security

- All data transmission uses HTTPS encryption
- Cookies are encrypted at rest
- Access tokens are time-limited and auto-expire
